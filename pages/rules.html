Trading Rules

1. No margin.
2. Cash-secured puts and covered calls only.
3. No trading around earnings or news events.
4. Document rationale for every trade.
5. Maintain 25% cash minimum.
6. Maximum 15% per position.
7. Maximum 20% per sector.
8. Maximum 2% risk per trade.
9. Target 4% monthly return.
10. Zero-day rule: would I open this position today?
11. Only enter new positions with >100% annualized return.
12. If underwater, sell covered calls at cost basis for >50% annualized.
13. Roll for credit when possible (14-30 days out, lower strike).
14. VIX-based deployment (VIX >25 = reduce exposure).
